Be at home even when you are not at home

Smartlock combines with FuebApp, an intelligently designed phone application that lets you control your safety with just one touch. It provides you with an efficient & smart way of monitoring & controlling the well-being of your home while creating a systematic interaction experience with automated notifications, in/out activity & so much more.

Minimal design, maximal safety.

Smartlock is designed to complement your way of life. It combines traditional with modern to provide you with maximum security. With 4 levels of authentication, it gives you the freedom to explore & choose from conventional Manual key lock to contemporary NFC, PinPad or Biometric options.

Satin Nickel Finish

Designed to embed & enhance your choice of style, Smartlock comes with a Satin Nickel Finish which gives a premium feel to the product and adds a layer of protective covering over it.
