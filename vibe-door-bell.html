Vibe Video Door Bell

You are always in control

Protect your home, see and talk to everyone who is at your doorstep through our modern looking Wi-Fi based and cloud connected video doorbell solution that can be installed at the front door or gate to look after your house.

Get a clear view of your home entrance day & night with wide angle view, high definition video and audio streaming to your smartphone app. Easy replacement of existing doorbell solutions.

A smart home for a safer life.

Experience the power of an intelligently designed Vibe bell with inventive proximity sensor technology which activates the camera and triggers instant mobile alerts when any suspicious movement is detected.

Two-way communication

See everything in your doorstep - from people to products or parcels on thr ground, live or recorded straight on your phone screens.

Wide Angle HD Camera

Adding to the experience is the wide-angle HD Camera, an intelligent window to the other side which gives you a clear view of the outer world through clear & crisp images & 720P HD video including at night.

LED Glass Rings

Microphone

Speakers
